About the Founder

As an artist and creative visionary, I've always believed that art has the power to connect souls, tell untold stories, and transform ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences. Growing up surrounded by Georgian culture, traditional craftsmanship, and the vibrant colors of our artistic heritage, I discovered my passion for bringing artists together.

My journey began with a simple question: How can we create a space where authentic art thrives, where emerging and established artists share the same stage, and where collectors discover pieces that speak to their hearts? That question became Ninart Vision.

About the Platform

Ninart Vision is an independent art platform dedicated to showcasing original, handcrafted artworks from talented Georgian and international artists. We are a curated collective where creativity knows no boundaries.
